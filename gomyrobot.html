About Go My Robot Academy

Located in the heart of Al Shorouk City, Egypt, Go My Robot Academy is dedicated to providing exceptional educational experiences in robotics, arts, and languages.

Our unique approach embraces the philosophy that "Learning is not LINEAR" - we adapt to each student's pace and learning style, ensuring everyone reaches their full potential.

With certified instructors, modern facilities, and a proven track record of success, we've become the most recommended academy in our community.
